Variants

Marauder can be replaced by Berserker, Knight, or Soldier, though soldier and knight will perform considerably worse. Sigil of Blood can provide an extra 200 hp/s. The slot can be changed to any utility you like: and are both good for defense, but keep for DPS.

This build is especially flexible, as long as you keep Radiance you can change Valor to whatever traits you want. If you get rid of Valor, dump the shield for a Focus.
