Power Vindicator

Death from above!

revenant (Series)
  1. Power Vindicator

This is a pretty foundational build for Revenant. Simple, solid and focused.

Gearing

  • Dragon's Helm, and full Berserker's otherwise, with Dragonhunter's runes
    • Alternates: Full Berserker's and/or Scholar runes
  • Sword/Sword with Force/Air Sigils
  • Staff with Force/Air Sigils
  • Relic of the Thief
  • Consumables:
    • Cilantro Lime Sous-Vide Steak (Power/Ferocity)
    • Superior Sharpening Stone (Power)

Notes

Power Vindicator can be very easy to play, yet deliver respectable results by following the steps below:

/Swap your legend when it is off cooldown or when you run out of energy, it doesn't matter much, and use Impossible Odds while in Shiro, and Vengeful Hammers while in Jalis. You can bind both these skills to the same button to reduce mistakes.

Use off cooldown in following priority:

  1. Sword 5 Deathstrike
  2. Sword 4 Shackling Wave
  3. Sword 2 Chilling Isolation

Dodge to start the fight and dodge when your endurance is almost full.

Auto attack to fill.

Crowd Control

Your CC skills are:

  • Jalis - Forced Engagement
  • Shiro - Jade Winds
  • Sword 4 Shackling Wave

However, if you're in a position where not CCing will result in failure, just swap to staff and use Staff 5 Surge of the Mists. Note that this will tank your DPS and the only thing you can do now is to auto attack (while still dodging and swapping legends).
